"NOOR" Meaning:

"light", "The Divine Light". An-Noor, meaning "the light" in Arabic.

“AL-HIDAYA” Meaning:

Hidayah is an Arabic word meaning Guidance. Guidance to the straight path, a path that directs us to the desired destination Jannah (Paradise). We constantly ask Allah (SWT) for guidance seventeen times each day in our five daily prayers. “Guide us to the straight path”
